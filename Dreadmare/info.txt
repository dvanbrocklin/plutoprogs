Dreadmare is a new game that I have been working on. It uses
LWJGL 2.9.3 and OpenGL 3.2. This is a 3D exploration game
similar to minecraft, but without blocks. As of right now it
is in the Testing Phase still (dm-20210206).

ChangeLog:

=== Version dm-20210206 ===

Additions:
- Skybox. Now shows clouds
- Hilly Terrain
- Paths
- Ferns
- Tall Grass
- Gravity
- Fog in the distance

Changes:
- Movement: 
-- Look around using the mouse
-- W and S move forwards and backwards
-- A and D move sideways (Doesn't always work correctly)
-- SPACE to jump
- Tree Texture & Model
- New Loading Screen

Removed:
- The spinning Gray dragon
- The ability to fly under the map

=== Version dm-20210204 ===

Features:

- A Spinning Gray Dragon
- A Flat grassy Ground
- 1000 randomly placed Trees
- The Ability to Move along the X, Y, and Z axis using WASD, SPACE, and LSHIFT
- You can not currently look around
